medicines

To ensure the best possible drug-therapy management for inpatients and outpatients, the Pharmacy Department is in charge of the purchase, storage, dispensing, preparation, distribution, and inventory of medications. Through monitoring and assessment services provided by legally qualified and professionally competent pharmacists, the Department also promotes medication safety and efficacy in hospitals at all times. The Department is in charge of the logical selection, monitoring, dosing, and control of patients' overall drug therapy programs in collaboration with the Pharmacy and Therapeutics Committee.

total care

Total Care honors medical professionals who prioritize health care rather than illness. By offering wellness coaching and preventive care, these doctors go above and beyond to improve their patients' overall health. They also work with patients who have chronic conditions to better meet their care needs. Total Care offers coordinated, patient-centered care that is frequently more reasonably priced.
